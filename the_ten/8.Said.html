8. SA'ID IBN ZAYD RAA

Sa'id Ibn Zayd RAA's father was Zayd ibn Amr and his genealogy meets with the Prophet's in Ka'b. His patronymic was Abul-A'var. He was also called Abu Tur.

Sa'id RAA was raised according to the Haneef religion which his father Zayd taught him. He was quick to follow Prophet Muhammad PBUH.

He was married to Umar RAA's sister Fatima.

With the exception of Badr, Sa'id ibn Zayd RAA participated in the battle at Uhud, the Ditch and all other battles.

Sa'id RAA spent the last days of his life on his farm in the Aqiq valley outside of Medina and, surpassing the age of seventy, he died in 50 or 51 H.
